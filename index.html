About

I spent 10 years building production deep learning systems at Eurailscout — national-scale railway inspection across optical, ultrasound, and eddy-current sensors. Published researcher at ECNDT 2018 (first author) and WCNDT 2016. M.Sc. Computational Science & Engineering from TU Munich.

Now focused on agentic LLM systems and cloud-native data engineering — bridging foundation models with real-world data pipelines. I build end-to-end: from multi-sensor data infrastructure through model training to production deployment on AWS.
